Use DreckTube

Its actually really simple!

First, go and join any DreckRoom by clicking on one of the listed boxes

You could also create your own DreckRoom for you and your friends by clicking on the button and entering a name

After you joined a DreckRoom

You should see these buttons on the left of your screen when you move your cursor across the website

Lets go through each one real quick

- Will change the DreckRoom to a smaller layout

- Will indicate whether you are Host of the DreckRoom. If so, it'll be gold, else it'll be just plain white

- Will open an inputbox to enter a URL or choose a file to stream

- Will send your current clipboard text to the server, if it's a valid URL it'll be added to the playlist

- Will show the current playlist on hover or click

- Will play the next video in the playlist

- Will show the current userlist on hover or click

- Will open the chat

- Will open a menu that lets you set the maximum threshhold to the current Host. If you are Host, it'll be the interval at which you update the rooms current time
